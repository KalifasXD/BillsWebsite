    Easy Encryption

    Cryptography / Security

    The Easy Encryption System is a secure platform for storing and sharing files with confidence. It protects sensitive data using advanced encryption, ensuring only authorized users can access files. Users create accounts with secure logins, and every file uploaded is automatically encrypted.     Enforcing Access Control Rules using Digital Rights Management Technology

    Access Control Systems

    The thesis titled Enforcing Access Control Rules Using Digital Rights Management Technology by Vasileios Dounavis, supervised by Georgios Lioudakis, focuses on implementing access control mechanisms to safeguard digital resources, particularly concerning copyright and trademarks. The research introduces a proof-of-concept system that employs Digital Rights Management (DRM) technology to maintain control over downloaded content, preventing unauthorized distribution and usage. By enforcing access restrictions even after content has been downloaded, the system aims to protect content creators and providers from unauthorized sharing and infringement. This approach highlights the potential of DRM technology in redefining and strengthening access control measures for digital content.
