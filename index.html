Who Am I?

My name is Vasileios, though most people call me Bill. I am a 23-year-old software engineer with a keen interest in cybersecurity and web development. My passion for coding goes beyond just one area; I enjoy building both the frontend—the user experience—and the backend—the functionality—of websites. My fascination with cybersecurity also inspired my thesis, where I explored this field in depth.

Programming is a true passion of mine, and I invest myself fully in every project, whether it's an application, website, or video game. My love for video games sparked my curiosity about how they're created, leading me to start using Unreal Engine 4-5 and Unity3D about four years ago. This experience even brought me into a partnership with Epic Games, the renowned creator of Fortnite.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy staying active by working out, spending time with friends, and, of course, playing video games.

Lastly, I was honored to serve my country starting in November 2023 and was discharged in August 2024. This experience taught me a great deal, rounding out my personal and professional development.
